About Perizinto Hub

Perizinto Hub is a digital catalogue manager built specifically for modern tailors and fashion designers.

Instead of sending blurry pictures on WhatsApp, this app lets you create a professional online shop link. Your customers can view your designs, check prices, and see measurements in full HD.

Why Go Premium?

  • Unlimited Uploads: Break free from the 10-design limit.
  • Video Portfolios: Add YouTube video links to show the fabric flow.
  • Direct QR Codes: Generate a scan code for your physical shop wall.
  • Quick Edit: Update prices and sizes on the fly.
